1.1.3 Data template exchange

ProcessSenderReceiver
31-10 00 00 Inception Phase

The "Data Template exchange" defines the structure of templates that can be used for the definition of the information exchange related to construction objects. Data templates essentially contain the definitions of characteristics and their grouping for a specific purpose. This standard does not specify specific property definitions, but rather provides the basis for stakeholders to agree among themselves to exchange property definitions and their grouping according to ISO 16739.

This exchange definition allows the configuration of cardinality options for each IFC entities and the creation of all fundamental concepts required during Data Templates exchange. This exchange definition includes, non-exhaustively, all concepts related to units of measurement, materials, grouping of properties, information of related documents, etc.

The content of the Data Templates shall be loadable from digital templates based on EN ISO/DIS 23386 and ISO/DIS 23387.
